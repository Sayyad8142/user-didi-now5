# Play Store Assets Required

Before submitting to Google Play Console, ensure you have:

## Required Assets
- **App Icon**: 512×512 PNG (export from resources/icon.png)
- **Feature Graphic**: 1024×500 PNG (promotional banner)
- **Screenshots**: At least 2 phone screenshots (1080×1920+ recommended)
- **Privacy Policy URL**: Must be publicly accessible web URL

## App Details
- **Title**: Didi Now — Instant Maids & Cooks
- **Short Description**: Book maids, cooks & bathroom cleaning. Instant or scheduled, with live updates.
- **Category**: Lifestyle or Home & Garden

## Data Safety Requirements
Complete the Data Safety form in Play Console:
- Personal info collected: name, phone
- App activity: bookings (service type, time, status)
- Optional files: admins may upload worker photos
- Data sharing: shared only with assigned worker for service fulfillment
- Security: encrypted in transit, row-level security
- Deletion: users can delete account and data from Profile → Account & Data

## App Access Notes
If your app requires phone verification:
- Provide test credentials for review
- Explain OTP verification process in review notes
